  • Who Are We - My team

    We are design freaks who admire beauty and like to indulge in making the minutest of details look good.

    Dwelling in BITS PILANI, the mundane college curriculum got too much on us so we decided to go bonkers instead. When the people around us were busy finding the unknown 'x', we took to finding out what art was about. Fascination driven by marvels that Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML, CSS, Javascript could produce made the choice simple. It was during the crazy hours of coding websites and designing posters, banners, logos, certificates, magazines for college fests that this fascination transformed into our passion.


    The works we produce are designed carefully to suit the purpose of make. Beautiful results with intuitive appeal are created by blending science and aesthetics in precise proportion. We work with ideas which remain invariably green and the on-going trends are subtly incorporated to pamper the immediate audience as well. Our technology is updated every instant to keep pace with the ever changing culture. After all:

    "Everything is designed. Few things are designed well" - Brian Reed
